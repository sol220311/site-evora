Meet the Baker

Hi, I'm Flávia, a proud Brazilian, wife, and mother of two little girls. Becoming a mom inspired me to start my own business, and it has truly been one of the best decisions of my life. Through this journey, I found the perfect way to blend my love for family with my passion for creating delicious, personalized brigadeiros. Every sweet treat I make is crafted with care, bringing a taste of Brazil and joy to every occasion.
