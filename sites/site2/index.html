What is a Brigadeiro?

The brigadeiro is a beloved Brazilian sweet made with condensed milk, butter, and cocoa powder, rolled into a ball and covered with sprinkles. It's a treat enjoyed at birthdays, weddings, and special moments, bringing joy to people of all ages.

Discover Flávia's unique take on this traditional dessert, with flavors ranging from classic to modern twists!
