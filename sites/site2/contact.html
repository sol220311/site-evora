Contact Us

We’d love to hear from you! If you’re interested in placing an order or have any questions, please feel free to reach out.

Order Information:

  • Box with 6 units (1 flavor): $15.00
  • Box with 24 units (up to 3 flavors): $40.00
  • Box with 70 units (up to 4 flavors): $110.00

How to Order:

  1. Check availability for your desired date (minimum 7 days in advance).
  2. Specify the quantity and flavors you’d like to order. For personalized brigadeiros, provide the event theme (if any), preferred colors, figures, and up to 4 letters for decoration.
  3. Make a 50% deposit via bank transfer to confirm your order.
  4. Agree on a pickup or delivery time on the agreed day.
  5. Wait for the big day and enjoy!
